Plant Care Guide

Expert tips and detailed directions to help your unique botanical varieties flourish perfectly inside your home green space.

Carnivorous Plants

These spectacular specimens require special pure fluid care. Emulate their natural swamp environments.

💧

Watering

Mineral-free distilled water or gathered rainwater only.

☀️

Light

Full direct sunshine or highly bright filtered light.

🌱

Soil

Nutrient-free pure moss mixtures or perlite blends.

🦟

Feeding

No fertilizers. Let them catch wild insects naturally.

Indoor Plants

Classic house interior selections thrive when provided bright, indirect open windows spaces.

💧

Watering

Allow top layers to dry thoroughly between sessions.

🌬️

Ventilation

Good ambient airflow and stable room temperatures.

✨

Maintenance

Wipe leaves with a damp cloth occasionally.

🔄

Rotation

Turn the container a quarter circle every month.

Tropical Species

Jungle varieties love elevated air moisture levels and require structural aeration for healthy root run.

🌡️

Humidity

Place a room humidifier near your shelves.

🌱

Soil

Use coconut coir, bark particles, and perlite.

❄️

Insulation

Shield carefully from sudden cold air drafts.

🧪

Nutrition

Feed lightly with diluted organic plant food.
